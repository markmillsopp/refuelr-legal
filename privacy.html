Privacy Policy

Effective: 5 May 2026

Refuelr is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, when data is used for tracking, and your choices.

Third-party content and attributions

Refuelr contains, shows, or accesses third-party content and services. This includes map data from OpenStreetMap contributors, official fuel price data published under the UK Open Government Licence v3.0, app store payment and subscription systems, and third-party services such as Firebase, RevenueCat, and Google Mobile Ads.

  • OpenStreetMap - map data © OpenStreetMap contributors, available under the Open Database License (ODbL).
  • UK fuel price data - official fuel price information is shown under the Open Government Licence v3.0.
  • Brand names and logos - station and supplier names, logos, and marks remain the property of their respective owners and are used only to identify relevant businesses or services.

What we collect

  • Name and Email Address for account creation and sign-in.
  • Precise Location to find nearby stations and support route-based features.
  • Search History (for example, saved routes and related route metadata) to provide core app features across devices when you are signed in.
  • User ID (for example Firebase UID) to operate authentication, data sync, and subscriptions.
  • Device ID (for example advertising identifier and push token) for advertising, analytics, and push delivery.
  • Purchase History (subscription/entitlement status) to enable paid features.
  • Product Interaction data to understand feature usage and improve app reliability.
  • Advertising Data (for example ad impressions/clicks and measurement signals) for ad serving and ad performance measurement.
  • Crash Data and Performance Data to diagnose and fix technical issues.

How we use your data

We use the data above for the following purposes, depending on data type: app functionality, analytics, and (where applicable) third-party advertising. We do not sell personal data to data brokers.

  • App Functionality: authentication, saved routes, subscriptions, security checks, and notifications.
  • Analytics: understanding usage patterns, app quality, and performance.
  • Third-Party Advertising: showing ads and measuring ad effectiveness for non-premium users.

Tracking and advertising

On iOS, Refuelr includes Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission prompt. If you allow tracking, advertising-related data (including certain device identifiers and ad measurement signals) may be used by advertising partners to provide and measure ads.

If you do not allow tracking, Refuelr will continue to function, but ad personalisation and ad measurement may be limited.

User privacy choices

  • iOS tracking permission: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • Manage notification permissions in device settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us.
  • If available in your region, use platform controls such as Limit Ad Tracking / personalised ads controls.

Third-party services

Refuelr uses third-party processors that may handle data described in this policy:

  • Firebase (Google) - authentication, database, cloud functions, messaging, and app analytics/diagnostics.
  • RevenueCat - subscription management and entitlement tracking.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) - ad delivery and ad measurement for non-premium users.

Community submissions

When you submit fuel or oil price information to Refuelr, that content may be reviewed, stored, and displayed to other users as part of the app experience. We may remove submissions that are inaccurate, abusive, infringing, misleading, or otherwise inconsistent with our terms or legal obligations.

Subscriptions & payments

Subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). Refuelr does not directly collect payment card numbers. Refunds are managed by the relevant app store.

Data retention

We retain account data while your account is active and for a limited period where needed for legal, fraud-prevention, or operational reasons. You can request account deletion. Diagnostic and operational logs may be retained for a limited period necessary for security and reliability.

International transfers

Some service providers may process data outside your country. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

Your rights

Changes to this policy
